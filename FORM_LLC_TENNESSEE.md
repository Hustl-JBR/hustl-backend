## ‚úÖ Why Form an LLC?

**Legal Protection:**
- **Separates personal assets from business debts**
- If business gets sued, your personal assets (house, car, savings) are protected
- Business debts stay with the business (not you personally)

**Professional:**
- Looks more legitimate to customers
- Required for business bank account (usually)
- Required for Stripe business account
- Better for taxes (pass-through taxation)

**Cost:**
- Only $50 minimum (very affordable!)
- One-time fee to form, small annual fee

**Recommendation:** ‚úÖ **YES - Form an LLC for legal protection**

## üéØ Step-by-Step: Form LLC in Tennessee

### Step 1: Check Name Availability (5 minutes)

**Go to:** https://tnbear.tn.gov/Ecommerce/FilingSearch.aspx

**Search for:**
- "Hustl LLC"
- "Hustl"
- "Hustl, LLC"

**What to Check:**
- Make sure it's available (not already taken)
- Should show "No results found" or "Available"

**If Taken:**
- Try: "Hustl Marketplace LLC"
- Try: "Hustl Platform LLC"
- Try: "Hustl App LLC"

**Once Available:** ‚úÖ You're good to proceed!

---

### Step 2: Get EIN (Recommended - 5 minutes, FREE)

**Why Get EIN First:**
- You'll need it for business bank account
- Required for Stripe business account
- Needed for taxes
- Free and instant!

**How to Get:**
1. Go to: https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-ein-online
2. Click "Apply Online Now"
3. Select: "Limited Liability Company" (even if you haven't formed it yet)
4. Select: "Domestic"
5. Fill out form:
   - **Business Name:** "Hustl LLC" (or whatever you choose)
   - **Business Address:** Your home address (for now)
   - **Name:** Your name
   - **SSN:** Your Social Security Number
   - **Business Purpose:** "Marketplace platform connecting service providers with customers"
6. Submit
7. **Get EIN instantly!** ‚úÖ
8. **Save the confirmation letter (PDF)** - you'll need it!

**EIN Format:** 12-3456789 (similar to SSN format)

**Time:** 5 minutes  
**Cost:** FREE ‚úÖ

---

### Step 3: File Articles of Organization (15 minutes, $50)

**Go to:** https://tnbear.tn.gov/Ecommerce/FilingSearch.aspx

**Or Direct Link:** https://tnbear.tn.gov/Ecommerce/FilingSearch.aspx

**Steps:**

1. **Click "File Online" or "Articles of Organization"**
   - Look for "Articles of Organization - Limited Liability Company"

2. **Fill Out Form:**
   
   **Business Information:**
   - **Business Name:** "Hustl LLC" (or your chosen name)
   - **Business Purpose:** "To operate a marketplace platform connecting service providers with customers seeking services"
   - **Principal Office Address:** Your home address (can be PO Box later)
     - Street Address
     - City: Knoxville (or your city)
     - State: Tennessee
     - ZIP Code

   **Registered Agent:**
   - **Name:** Your name
   - **Address:** Same as principal office (your home address)
   - **Phone:** Your phone number
   - **Email:** Your email address
   - **Note:** You can be your own registered agent (free!)

   **Management:**
   - **Type:** Member-Managed (you manage it)
   - **Member Name:** Your name
   - **Member Address:** Your address

   **Effective Date:**
   - Usually "Immediately" or today's date

   **Duration:**
   - Usually "Perpetual" (unlimited duration)

3. **Review Information**
   - Double-check everything is correct
   - Make sure spelling is correct

4. **Pay Filing Fee**
   - **Cost:** $50 minimum (per member)
   - Since you're the only member: $50
   - Payment: Credit/debit card online

5. **Submit**
   - Submit form
   - Get confirmation email
   - Save confirmation number!

**Time:** 15-30 minutes  
**Cost:** $50 (one-time)  
**Processing:** Usually 1-2 business days

---

### Step 4: Wait for Confirmation (1-2 Business Days)

**You'll Receive:**
- Email confirmation from Tennessee Secretary of State
- Certificate of Organization (official document)
- Confirmation number

**Check Status:**
- Go to: https://tnbear.tn.gov/Ecommerce/FilingSearch.aspx
- Search for "Hustl LLC"
- Should show as "Active" once approved

**If Rejected:**
- Usually due to name conflict or missing information
- You'll get email explaining why
- Fix issue and resubmit (usually no extra fee if quick)

---

### Step 5: Create Operating Agreement (Recommended - 30 minutes)

**What It Is:**
- Document that explains how your LLC will be run
- Not required by Tennessee, but **highly recommended**
- Protects you legally
- Shows you're running business properly

**What to Include:**
- Business name and address
- Member names (yours)
- Management structure (member-managed)
- How profits/losses are shared (100% to you)
- How decisions are made (by you)
- How to add/remove members (later if needed)
- Dissolution procedures (if you close business)

**How to Create:**

**Option 1: Use Template (FREE)**
- Go to: https://www.nolo.com/legal-encyclopedia/llc-operating-agreement.html
- Download Tennessee LLC Operating Agreement template
- Fill in blanks (your info)
- Sign it (you sign as member)
- **Cost:** FREE ‚úÖ

**Option 2: Use Service (PAID)**
- LegalZoom: ~$99
- Rocket Lawyer: ~$40/month
- Northwest Registered Agent: ~$225

**Option 3: Have Lawyer Create ($300-500)**
- Best if you have complex situation
- Worth it for peace of mind

**Recommendation:** Start with free template, upgrade later if needed.

**Keep Operating Agreement:**
- Save PDF copy
- Print physical copy
- Keep with business records

---

### Step 6: Open Business Bank Account (Same Day - 1 Week)

**Requirements:**
- ‚úÖ EIN letter (from IRS) - **You need this!**
- ‚úÖ LLC Certificate (from TN Secretary of State)
- ‚úÖ Operating Agreement (if you created one)
- ‚úÖ Driver's license
- ‚úÖ Opening deposit ($50-500, depends on bank)

**Where to Go:**
- **Local Bank/Credit Union:** Best service, support local
- **Chase Business:** Good nationwide
- **Bank of America Business:** Good nationwide
- **Online:** Mercury, Novo (tech-friendly)

**What to Bring:**
- EIN confirmation letter (PDF - print it)
- LLC Certificate (PDF - print it)
- Operating Agreement (if you have it)
- Driver's license
- Opening deposit check/cash

**Ask Bank:**
- "I need to open a business checking account for my LLC"
- They'll guide you through process
- Usually takes 30-60 minutes

**Account Name:** "Hustl LLC" (not your personal name)

**Cost:** Usually FREE (some banks charge $10-25/month, ask first!)

**Time:** Same day (if you have all documents)

---

### Step 7: Update Stripe Business Profile (1 Day)

**Now That You Have LLC:**

1. **Go to Stripe Dashboard:**
   - https://dashboard.stripe.com/settings/business

2. **Update Business Information:**
   - **Business Name:** "Hustl LLC"
   - **Business Type:** "LLC" or "Limited Liability Company"
   - **EIN:** Your EIN number (12-3456789)
   - **Business Address:** Your LLC address
   - **Business Bank Account:** Connect your new business account

3. **Upload Documents:**
   - LLC Certificate (from TN Secretary of State)
   - EIN letter (from IRS)
   - Operating Agreement (if available)

4. **Complete Verification:**
   - Stripe will verify your business
   - Usually takes 1-2 business days
   - You'll get email confirmation

**Why Important:**
- Required for Stripe Connect (hustler payouts)
- Looks more professional
- Better for taxes
